Text: July 16: So reality is as temporary and illusory as that firm ground feeling, in that split second it seems an unshakable truth that the ground is solid. Is it forced ? Is it an illusion ? Is it a conscious trickery of the mind where we willingly play the part ? It is only, at times, but a thin veil cast in front of our eyes. on rare occasions, with the lucidity of a drunk man who for a moment holds on to his tanking boat of a sidewalk, we glimpse at the timid creature in the woods.
